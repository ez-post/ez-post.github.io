CUSTOMIZABLE POSTCARDS

We let you customize your own postcard, turn it into a physical postcard and directly send it to the designated address at an affordable price.

We will take care of all your hassle from buying a postcard to delivering it at the post office. With that, you can actually focus more on travelling during your vacation and less on nitty-gritty logistic.

4 inch X 6 inch postcard.

Customizable photo on front side of the postcard.

User message and recipient address on the back.

Mailing and stamp included (Malaysia-Penang Stamp).

Cost: RM 4.99 nett / each card.

SHIPPING POLICY

We commit to deliver our postcard to both local and international address within 14 working days subject to Pos Malaysia delivery time.

RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

In the event of postcard not received in 21 working days, EZ Post will resend postcard upon request by customer on a case by case basis. Postcard is considered received by recipient after 30 working days after payment is made.
