CUSTOMIZABLE POSTCARDS

We let you customizable your own post card, print it into hard copy and directly send it to the designated address at an affordable price.

We will take care of all hassle from buying a post card to delivering it at post office. With that, you can actually focus more on travelling during your vacation and less on nitty-gritty logistic.

4 inch X 6 inch post card

Customizable photo on front side

User message and recipient address on the back

Mailing and stamp included (Malaysia-Penang Stamp)
