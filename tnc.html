PRINTING POLICY

Postcard preview in app and postcard printed might differ slightly due to handphone brightness settings. It is advisable to take photo under outdoor setting or under good lighting to have the best printing effect.

SHIPPING POLICY

We commit to deliver our postcard to local address within 2 weeks and international address within 4 weeks subject to Pos Malaysia delivery time.

RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

In the event of postcard not received in 4 weeks, EZ Post will resend postcard upon request by customer on a case by case basis. Postcard is considered received by recipient after 6 weeks after payment is made.
