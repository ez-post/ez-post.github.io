EZ-Post makes your travel gift memorable and unique by turning your beautiful photo into a postcard with simple few click on EZ Post app

The Story of EZ Post

Where It All Begins

I am fortunate to have a chance to visit New Zealand, I was captivated by New Zealand stunning snow cap mountain, the great lake and the natural beauty of its landscape.

Doing What Budget Traveller Do Best

Like all the typical traveller visiting a new place, I am very good at snapping photos and before long, I already have thousands of photos in my phone. At the same time, the souvenir being offers in New Zealand is a bit pricy and it is generic. It is not so much related to my story in New Zealand. We struggle to decide what to purchase as a souvenir.

Lightbulb Moment

So, when we are thinking of what to gift for our friends and family. An idea came up that why not we make use of our beautiful photo to send as a postcard as we have thousands of that in the phone, it will be a waste to let it sit there.

Sharing with others

We wanted to share such conveniences with others and empower user to create their unique gift. So we created EZ Post. Personalized postcard can be easily send from anywhere at any time with free delivery worldwide at an affordable price. Once you downloaded the app, follow simple infographic below and get your first personalized card in no time.

USING EZ POST APP

STEP 1

Take a photo from your camera.

STEP 2

Provide a short message for your recipient.

STEP 3

Provide the full recipient address to send to.

STEP 4

Make online payment to EZ-post.

STEP 5

Postcard will reach recipient in Malaysia within 2 weeks. (Up to 4 weeks for the rest of the world)

NOTICE

We have a policy of resending postcard, if postcard was not reach within 6 weeks, tracking number is required for administrative purposes.
