SHIPPING POLICY

We commit to deliver our postcard to both local and international address within 4 weeks subject to Pos Malaysia delivery time.

RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

In the event of postcard not received in 4 weeks, EZ Post will resend postcard upon request by customer on a case by case basis. Postcard is considered received by recipient after 6 weeks after payment is made.

PRIVACY POLICY

  1. We do not share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop of our products, or protect our rights.
  2. We do not store personal information on our servers unless required for the ongoing operation of one of our services.
